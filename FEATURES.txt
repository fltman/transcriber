TRANSCRIBER - FEATURES

Upload, record or live-transcribe meetings and get a complete
transcript with speakers identified by name.

TRANSCRIPTION
- Upload audio or video files (MP3, MP4, WAV, WebM, M4A)
- Record directly in the browser with a visual audio level meter
- Choose which microphone to use, or capture computer audio
- Live mode: see the transcript appear word by word as you speak
- Optimized for Swedish using locally running AI models

SPEAKER IDENTIFICATION
- Automatically detects who is speaking and when
- Identifies speakers by name from meeting introductions
- Works even without introductions (labels speakers as Participant 1, 2, etc.)
- During live recording, speakers are identified progressively

EDITING
- Click any line to correct the text
- Move lines between speakers if they were assigned wrong
- Rename speakers, change their colors, or merge two into one
- Original text is always preserved alongside your edits

PLAYBACK
- Click any line to hear that part of the recording
- The transcript scrolls along automatically during playback

EXPORT
- Download transcripts in 7 formats: SRT, WebVTT, plain text,
  Markdown, JSON, Word (DOCX), and PDF
- Each format includes speaker names and timestamps

SMART ACTIONS
- Run AI-powered analyses on your transcripts: summaries, action
  item lists, meeting minutes, or any custom prompt you define
- Save your favorite prompts as reusable actions
- Browse and export results from previous runs

ENCRYPTION
- Protect sensitive transcripts with a password
- Encrypted meetings require the password to view or export

SETTINGS
- Choose between different AI models for each task
- Supports both cloud models and locally running models
- Settings are saved automatically

LIVE RECORDING
- Start a live session and see transcription in real time
- After you stop, the system automatically reprocesses with a
  higher-quality model for the best possible result
- Speaker names are refined in the background as more context
  becomes available

INTERFACE
- Clean dark interface with meeting list and status overview
- Three ways to start: upload a file, record, or go live
- Progress bar shows each step during processing
